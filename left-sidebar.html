About Hostels

KIET has good hostel facilities for both boys and girls. KIET Possesses 3 boys hostel & 3 girls hostel. In addition there are 2 off campus hostels for first year students. First year students (boys) reside outside the college campus in hostels to maintain ragging free environment of the college. It has the capacity to accommodate 325 students. The college hostels are well built and provide excellent facilities including computer corner with internet & WI-FI connection, 24 hours power backup. Recreation facilities like: TV room, billiard room, indoor games and well furnished gyms separately for boys and girls. The hostel messes are run with active cooperation and involvement of the students. Students get high quality, well balanced and nutritious vegetarian meal. KIET has students coming from various parts of the country; of which most of them belong to the states of Uttar Pradesh and New Delhi. Day scholars can avail institute buses running from various corner of Ghaziabad/Meerut.
